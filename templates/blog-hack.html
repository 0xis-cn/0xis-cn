Dr Gregory B Newby, CEO of Project Gutenberg Literary Archive Foundation of over twenty years, died after a short battle with cancer. He drove the release of 75,000+ ebooks as a key volunteer, co-created an AI-narrated audiobook collection honoured by TIME, and advanced supercomputing technology. His work on public access to literature will be deeply missed.
